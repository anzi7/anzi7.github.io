Hello! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Applied Mathematics at National Sun Yat-sen University, Taiwan, enrolled in the International PhD Program for Science (IPPS), expected to graduate in 2026, under the supervision of Jephian C.-H. Lin, Associate Professor, Department of Applied Mathematics, College of Science, NSYSU. I earned an M.Sc. in Mathematics from St. Joseph's University, India (2017) and a B.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics from Christ University, India (2014). Before my Ph.D., I completed formal teaching training and a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) from Manipur University (2025). My research focuses on spectral graph theory, combinatorial matrix theory, and inverse eigenvalue problems of graphs (IEPG). I am also interested in extremal spectral graph theory, matrix inertia, algebraic graph invariants, and applications to network analysis. I am currently seeking postdoctoral positions and open for collaborations in these areas.
